Stubble burning releases large amounts of particulate matter such as carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides and other pollutants into the atmosphere, deteriorating the air quality.
To address this issue, we have proposed Strawbusters - an innovative web portal to connect farmers to agricultural production based companies so that the companies can contact farmers directly and purchase the stubble.








We believe that Strawbusters is a sustainable and technologically-driven solution which will help in preventing air pollution and help farmers increase their revenue.
